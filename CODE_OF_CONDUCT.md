# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community for all contributors — regardless of age, background, experience level, or perspective.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback and discussion
- Welcoming contributions from people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- Focusing on what is best for learners and educators
- Being patient with contributors who are new to open source

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Adding content that is unsafe or inappropriate for children
- Adding commercial, partisan, or manipulative content to the curriculum
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues or pull requests

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting maintainers via GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately.
