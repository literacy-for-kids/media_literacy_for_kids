Welcome to **Media Literacy for Kids** — an 18-week, open-source curriculum that helps young learners (ages 8–12) navigate the information landscape and filter the signal from the noise.

<!-- truncate -->

The curriculum is organized into five units covering everything from what media *is*, to how algorithms shape what we see, to creating responsible media of your own.

Each week includes two guided sessions and one independent creative session — about 90 minutes of learning per week.

Head to the [Introduction](/docs/intro) to get started, or browse the full [Curriculum Overview](/docs/curriculum-overview) to see what's ahead.
